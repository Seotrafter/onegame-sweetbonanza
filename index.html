Frequently Asked Questions

What is Combo Clash?

Combo Clash is an innovative game that combines merge mechanics with tower defense gameplay. Progress through different historical eras while building and upgrading your army through strategic merging and deployment.

How does the gameplay work?

The game features two main mechanics: merging similar units to create stronger ones, and strategically placing these units to defend against enemy waves. You'll need to manage your resources and think strategically about unit placement and upgrades.

What makes the game unique?

Combo Clash stands out by combining idle gameplay with active tower defense elements. The historical progression through different eras adds depth, while the merge mechanics create an engaging upgrade system.

How does progression work?

As you advance through historical eras, you'll unlock new units and technologies. The game features both active gameplay through tower defense and idle progression, allowing you to earn resources even when offline.
